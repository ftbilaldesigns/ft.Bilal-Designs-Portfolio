About Me

Who Am I

I'm Bilal — a freelance graphic designer based in Kerala, India, with 3 years of hands-on experience crafting visuals that are clean, purposeful, and built to perform. Working in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, I bring a minimal, professional approach to every project — stripping away the noise so the design does the talking.

I'm open for project-based work and long-term creative collaborations — not as an employee, but as a dedicated creative partner. Whether you're a startup finding your visual voice, a small business building credibility, a content creator shaping your brand, a growing venture, an agency needing reliable support, or an individual who simply wants to look their best — I'm here for it.

Every pixel has a purpose. Every deliverable is crafted with intention — because great design isn't just about looking good, it's about communicating the right thing to the right people at the right time.
